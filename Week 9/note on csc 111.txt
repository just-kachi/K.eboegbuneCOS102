A complete set of IFRS financial statements consists of the following components:
1) Statement of profit or loss and other comprehensive income
2) Statement of changes in equity
3) Statement of financial position
4) Statement of cash flows
5) Notes to the financial statements, compromising a summary of significant accounting policies and
   other explanatory information

Assets = Capital + Liability

statement of cash flow shows the inflow and outflow of cash for a company

liquidity is the ability of an entity to meet up with their short term obiligation
